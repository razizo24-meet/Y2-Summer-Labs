How to Build Bad Habits and Leave Bad Ones

When it comes to habits, friction is key. What???

More friction for bad ones and less for good ones. In simpler words, make good habits as easy as possible with almost no obstacles and bad ones with as many obstacles as you can make them, so they are hard to reach.
